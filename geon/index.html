About Us

GLUG

We are a GNU/Linux Users Group associated with Free Software Movement Karnataka.

Freedom

We believe in Knowledge Freedom and encourage use of Free Software and Open Hardware.

Community

We love to contribute, share and spread awareness among people. We also have a lot of fun.

Who are we?

We are students and enthusiasts coming together for a cause. We work towards design freedom in electronic hardware and promote use of libre tools in academics, research and our daily lives.

We also stand for Digital Privacy, Gender Equality and Socio-political Awareness. To learn more, visit

Events

Sessions

We conduct weekly sessions at Government Engineering College, Hassan. Participants are involved in hands-on project developments, activities and philosophical discussions.

Workshops

We conduct workshops on electronics related topics that utilize Free Software and Open Hardware tools. The very first workshop on Arduino was conducted in Nov, 2016.

Regional Camp

We organize the GEON ENRIFS Camp in collaboration with GLUE GLUG every year in April. The tracks were Embedded systems in 2017, IoT in 2018, Drone in 2019.

Localization Sprint

We contributed to the Kannada Rajyotsava Localization Sprint 2017 organized by FSMK, as a part of team Hassan along with GLUE GLUG and RIT GLUG, from 15th Oct to 16th Nov.

FSMK Winter Camp

We were volunteers for the Open-Hardware Track of FSMK Winter Camp at RIT, Hassan in 2018 and at PESCE, Mandya in 2019. On both occasions the topic was Internet of Things.

Awareness Drives

We visit schools to enlighten the students and teachers on technology, promote Free Software among them as well as engage in discussions and activites with them.

Competitions

We organize competitions such as Women's Day Poster Designing and Techie Train to allow the participants to showcase their talents and reward the best ones.
